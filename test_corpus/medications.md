# Current Medications

## Daily Medications
- **Warfarin** 5mg - Take once daily in the evening. Blood thinner for atrial fibrillation.
- **Lisinopril** 10mg - Take once daily in the morning. ACE inhibitor for blood pressure.
- **Metformin** 500mg - Take twice daily with meals. For type 2 diabetes management.

## As-Needed Medications
- **Acetaminophen** (Tylenol) 500mg - For pain relief. Max 3000mg per day.
- **Loratadine** (Claritin) 10mg - For seasonal allergies.

## Important Warnings
- Do NOT take ibuprofen or aspirin with Warfarin — increased bleeding risk.
- Do NOT take NSAIDs (Advil, Aleve, ibuprofen) — interacts with both Warfarin and Lisinopril.
- Avoid grapefruit juice — interacts with Warfarin.
