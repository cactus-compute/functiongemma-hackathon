# Allergies

## Drug Allergies
- **Penicillin** — Severe reaction: hives and throat swelling. Diagnosed 2019.
- **Sulfa drugs** (Sulfamethoxazole) — Moderate reaction: skin rash. Diagnosed 2021.
- **Codeine** — Mild reaction: nausea and dizziness.

## Food Allergies
- **Shellfish** — Moderate: stomach cramps, hives. Carry EpiPen.
- **Tree nuts** (walnuts, pecans) — Mild: itchy mouth, manageable.

## Environmental Allergies
- Dust mites — Year-round, managed with Claritin
- Ragweed — Seasonal (Aug-Oct)
